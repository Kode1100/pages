The Netherlands aid vs Corruption Index

Together with Egon Hollands (and a little help from @davetaz) we have created a visualisation of 4 main data sources:

  1. The amount of aid given by the Netherlands to recipient countries by date.
  2. Transparency International ‘Corruption Perceptions Index’ by country, per year.
  3. Gapminder World regions colors.
  4. Population data, by country from the World Bank.

We have encountered the typical problems with data analysis:

First, the laptop has crashed for suspicious reasons. We think it was the NSA.
Secondly, we had to filter out data that was not relevant to our scope (name of the project, countries not receiving aid etc)
Then we had to do the cleanup and refine:
ensure that the dates and years were in a compatible format
manually fix some country names (North Korea vs Democratic People's Republic of Korea vs Korea (North))
then we had to aggregate the disbursements to each country per year
we had to manually fix an error in the data for Mali by cross-referencing with data from the Dutch Ministry of Foreign Affairs
we had to estimate population for 2013 as the data is not yet available from World Bank.
